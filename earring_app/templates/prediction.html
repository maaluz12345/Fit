Understanding Calorie Burn Prediction

Calorie burn prediction refers to estimating the number of calories your body expends during various activities. This can be useful for:

Weight Management: Whether you want to lose, gain, or maintain your weight, knowing how many calories you burn can help you achieve your goals.

Fitness Planning: By understanding your calorie expenditure, you can better plan your workouts and adjust your diet accordingly.

Personalized Insights: Our app provides tailored insights based on your personal information and activity level, helping you make informed choices.

Activity Tracking: You can monitor how different exercises and daily activities contribute to your total calorie burn.
